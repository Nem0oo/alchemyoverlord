Rager IBU Calculator

This page provides a calculator for predicting IBUs using the Jackie Rager formula from an article in Zymurgy (vol. 13, no. 4, pp. 54-54, 1990). This formula is provided at Norm Pyle's Hops FAQ.

The only way to really know the IBU level of a beer is to have it professionally tested, which is something I highly recommend. Cheers!
