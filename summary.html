Summary

The link(s) on the left provide calculators for various beer-related computations. At this point, there's (a) an IBU calculator using the Tinseth formula, (b) a calculator for the mIBU method described on my blog, (c) a page for estimating the hop freshness factor and HSI, and (d) a tutorial on perceptual difference testing using interactive worksheets. Hopefully in the future there will be more.

The source code (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) for these calculators is available by clicking on the "source code" link above.

The "blog" link above will take you to my blog, which is all about experimenting with the process of making beer.
